We don’t just make films.
We reclaim our stories.

For too long, African narratives have been told by outsiders, shaped by a foreign gaze. Mbogiwood was founded not just as a production house, but as a reclamation—a declaration that our stories, in all their complexity and beauty, deserve to be told by us.
