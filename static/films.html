Supa Modo

Her greatest power was believing.

A young girl's terminal illness inspires her village to fulfill her dream of becoming a superhero, proving that the power of community can make anyone fly.

Watch Now

Synopsis

Jo, a witty 9-year-old girl, is terminally ill. As a way of dealing with her diagnosis, she retreats into a fantasy world of superheroes. Her single mother Kathryn is torn between her daughter’s realistic chances and her desire to make Jo’s last days as joyful as possible.

When Jo is sent home from the hospital, her older sister and the entire village conspire to make her dreams come true, turning their small rural town into a film set where Jo gets to be the star. 'Supa Modo' is a touching and imaginative story about the resilience of the human spirit.
